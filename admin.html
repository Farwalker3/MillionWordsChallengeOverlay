2Review Submissions

In your Google Sheet, you'll see all story submissions with these columns:

  • Timestamp - When submitted
  • Twitch Username - Who submitted
  • Story Title - Title of the story
  • Genre - Optional genre
  • Your Story - The story content
  • Status - Dropdown: Pending/Approved/Rejected
  • Word Count - Auto-calculated (must be 50+)
  • Approved Date - When you approved it
  • Notes - Your moderation notes

3Approve or Reject Stories

For each new submission:

  1. Read the story content
  2. Check the word count (must be 50+)
  3. Verify it's PG-friendly (no profanity, violence, mature content)
  4. In the Status column, select:
    • Approved - Story will appear on stream
    • Rejected - Story will not appear
  5. If approved, enter today's date in Approved Date column
  6. Optionally add notes in the Notes column

4Ban Users (If Needed)

To ban a user from submitting stories:

  1. Create a new sheet tab called "Banned Users"
  2. List banned usernames (one per row in column A)
  3. Manually check new submissions against this list
  4. Reject all stories from banned users
